**CATEGORY 4: Cybersecurity & Safety**

**Q: Do I really need antivirus?**
A: Yes. Modern threats are real. A good antivirus is basic protection. We set up industry-trusted software that won't slow your device down.

**Q: What's 2FA? Do I need it?**
A: 2FA (two-factor authentication) adds a second security step when you log in. It's especially important for email, banking, and Apple/Google accounts. We set it up and teach you.

**Q: How do I spot a phishing scam?**
A: Common red flags: urgent language, asking for passwords, suspicious links, too-good-to-be-true offers. We run education sessions on this.

**Q: What should I do if I think I've been hacked?**
A: Change your password immediately. Enable 2FA. Check account activity. We can help you secure everything. Contact us ASAP.

### Conversion Flow

```
Visitor arrives
    ↓
Problem-solution match (Home hero + problem cards)
    ↓
Service interest (explore Services or Pricing)
    ↓
Lead capture (Book Help form)
    ↓
Confirmation + scheduling
    ↓
Service delivery + upsell (subscription or AI chatbot)
```

### Key Conversion Principles

- **Every section has a CTA** (no dead ends)
- **Multiple CTA styles** (buttons, links, cards)
- **Friction-free lead capture** (name, phone, issue only)
- **Trust signals throughout** (Sydney local, transparent pricing, plain English)
- **Problem-before-solution** (meets users where they are)
- **AI chatbot as conversion tool** (guides users, qualifies leads)
- **Recurring revenue highlighted** (subscriptions, chatbot services)
